About Fuel Cost Calculator

The Fuel Cost Calculator estimates the total fuel expense for a road trip. Choose metric (km and L/100km) or imperial (miles and mpg) units, then enter the distance, fuel efficiency, and fuel price. The tool calculates total fuel volume used, total cost, and cost per kilometer or mile. It is useful for trip budgeting, comparing vehicles, and tracking travel expenses.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is fuel cost calculated (metric)?

Fuel Cost = (Distance / 100) x Fuel Efficiency x Price per Liter. Fuel efficiency in metric is liters per 100 km.

How is fuel cost calculated (imperial)?

Fuel Cost = (Distance / MPG) x Price per Gallon. Gallons = Miles / MPG.

Is this tool free?

Yes, completely free with no login required.
