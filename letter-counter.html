Text processing tasks that once required desktop software or manual effort can now be done instantly in the browser no installation, no account, and completely free to use as often as you need.

Whether you are formatting text for a blog post, cleaning up copied content, converting between different case styles, counting words for a submission limit, removing duplicates from a list, or sorting lines alphabetically, our Letter Counter handles the task accurately and instantly. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Letter Counter free to use?

Yes, completely free. Letter Counter requires no account, no subscription, and no payment. Use it as often as you like with no limits.

Is there a character or word limit in Letter Counter?

No. Letter Counter handles text of any length. Paste in as much content as you need and the tool processes it all instantly.

Does Letter Counter work in my browser without installation?

Yes. Letter Counter runs entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. No software, extension, or plugin installation is required.

Is my text kept private when I use Letter Counter?

Yes. All text processing in Letter Counter happens locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to or stored on any server.

Can I use Letter Counter on a mobile device?

Yes. Letter Counter is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets as well as desktop computers.
