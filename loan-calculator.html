About the Loan Calculator Tool

The Loan Calculator is a free online financial tool that helps you estimate monthly loan payments and understand the total cost of borrowing. Enter the loan amount, interest rate, and loan term to instantly calculate your monthly payment, total payment amount, and total interest. An amortization schedule shows the first 12 months of payments, breaking down how much goes toward principal versus interest each month. This loan calculator is useful for comparing mortgage offers, planning car purchases, evaluating personal loan options, and understanding how different interest rates affect your monthly payments. Whether you are buying a home, financing a vehicle, or consolidating debt, this tool helps you make informed borrowing decisions.
