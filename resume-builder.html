About the Resume Builder Tool

The Resume Builder is a free online tool for creating professional resumes. Fill in your personal information, add work experience and education entries, and list your skills. The resume preview updates in real time so you can see exactly how your resume will look when printed. You can add multiple work experience and education entries, and remove any you don't need. The Print button prints only the resume area, hiding the tool interface, making it easy to save as PDF or print a physical copy for job applications.
