Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vowel Counter free to use?

Yes, completely free. Vowel Counter requires no account, no subscription, and no payment. Use it as often as you like with no limits.

Is there a character or word limit in Vowel Counter?

No. Vowel Counter handles text of any length. Paste in as much content as you need and the tool processes it all instantly.

Yes. Vowel Counter runs entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. No software, extension, or plugin installation is required.

Yes. All text processing in Vowel Counter happens locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to or stored on any server.

Can I use Vowel Counter on a mobile device?

Yes. Vowel Counter is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets as well as desktop computers.
