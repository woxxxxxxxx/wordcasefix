About the Grade Calculator Tool

The Grade Calculator is a free online tool that helps students calculate their weighted grades based on assignments, exams, and projects. Simply add each assignment with its score percentage and weight, and the calculator automatically computes your overall grade. The built-in"what-if" feature lets you predict how a final exam score would affect your final grade, helping you set target scores for your goals. This weighted grade calculator is ideal for college and high school students tracking their performance throughout the semester, teachers quickly computing class grades, and parents monitoring their children's academic progress. The tool supports percentage-based grading systems and displays both your numeric grade and letter grade equivalent.
