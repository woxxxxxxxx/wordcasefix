About Number Sorter

The Number Sorter arranges a list of numbers in ascending or descending order. Numbers can be separated by spaces, commas, or newlines. After sorting, the tool shows key statistics: minimum, maximum, sum, average, and count. Non-numeric tokens are flagged and skipped. The output can be formatted with newlines, commas, or spaces.

Frequently Asked Questions

What separators are supported in the input?

You can use spaces, commas, and newlines -- or any combination. The tool splits on all whitespace and commas automatically.

Are decimal numbers supported?

Yes. Any valid decimal number (e.g. 3.14, -7.5, 1e6) is parsed and sorted correctly.

Is this tool free?

Yes, completely free with no login required.
