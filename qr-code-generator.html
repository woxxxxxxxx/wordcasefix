About the QR Code Generator Tool

The QR Code Generator is a free online tool that creates QR codes from any text or URL. Simply type or paste your content and a QR code is generated instantly. You can download the QR code as a PNG image for use on business cards, flyers, posters, websites, or product packaging. This free QR code maker uses the qrcode.js library and generates scannable QR codes compatible with all major QR code readers on smartphones and tablets.
