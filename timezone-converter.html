About the Timezone Converter Tool

The Timezone Converter lets you convert any date and time between 22 major world timezones covering every continent. Select your source and destination timezones, pick a date and time, and the converted time appears instantly. The page also shows a live world clock that updates every 10 seconds, displaying the current local time in all 22 cities simultaneously.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert a time to another timezone?

Select the source timezone, enter the date and time, and select the target timezone. The converted time appears automatically.

How often does the world clock update?

The world clock updates every 10 seconds to show current times accurately.

Is daylight saving time accounted for?

Yes, the converter uses your browser's built-in timezone data which includes DST rules.
