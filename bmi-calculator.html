About the BMI Calculator Tool

The BMI Calculator is a free online health tool that calculates your Body Mass Index using either metric or imperial units. Simply enter your height and weight, and the tool instantly computes your BMI with a color-coded visual range indicator that shows where you fall on the BMI scale, from underweight to obese. Along with the calculation, you receive personalized health advice based on your results. This body mass index calculator is useful for anyone tracking their fitness journey, consulting with healthcare professionals, or simply wanting to understand their weight status. The BMI calculator supports both centimeters and feet/inches for height, as well as kilograms and pounds for weight, making it accessible to users worldwide.
