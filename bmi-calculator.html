About the BMI Calculator Tool

The BMI Calculator is a free online health tool that calculates your Body Mass Index using either metric or imperial units. Simply enter your height and weight, click Calculate, and the tool instantly computes your BMI with a color-coded visual range indicator that shows where you fall on the BMI scale, from underweight to obese. Along with the calculation, you receive personalized health advice based on your results. This BMI calculator is useful for anyone tracking their fitness journey, consulting with healthcare professionals, or simply wanting to understand their weight status.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMI Calculator?

A BMI calculator is a free online tool that calculates your Body Mass Index based on your height and weight to help assess whether you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

How do I use this BMI Calculator?

Enter your height and weight using either metric or imperial units, then click Calculate. The tool displays your BMI value and the corresponding weight category.

Is this BMI Calculator free?

Yes, this tool is completely free to use with no signup required.
