About the UTM Builder

This free UTM Builder creates campaign tracking URLs with UTM parameters for Google Analytics. UTM parameters (utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign, utm_term, utm_content) allow you to identify the source and effectiveness of your marketing campaigns in Google Analytics reports. Required fields are source, medium, and campaign name.

Frequently Asked Questions

What do UTM parameters do?

UTM parameters are tags appended to a URL that tell Google Analytics where your traffic comes from. utm_source identifies the referrer (e.g., "google"), utm_medium identifies the marketing channel (e.g., "cpc"), and utm_campaign identifies the specific marketing initiative.
