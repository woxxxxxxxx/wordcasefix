Frequently Asked Questions

Is Capitalize Text free to use?

Yes, completely free. Capitalize Text requires no account, no subscription, and no payment. Use it as often as you like with no limits.

Is there a character or word limit in Capitalize Text?

No. Capitalize Text handles text of any length. Paste in as much content as you need and the tool processes it all instantly.

Does Capitalize Text work in my browser without installation?

Yes. Capitalize Text runs entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. No software, extension, or plugin installation is required.

Is my text kept private when I use Capitalize Text?

Yes. All text processing in Capitalize Text happens locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to or stored on any server.

Can I use Capitalize Text on a mobile device?

Yes. Capitalize Text is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets as well as desktop computers.
