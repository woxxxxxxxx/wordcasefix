About the Line Sorter

This free Line Sorter arranges lines of text in your chosen order. Sort alphabetically A to Z or Z to A, by line length (shortest or longest first), numerically for number lists, or randomly for shuffling. Additional options let you perform case-insensitive sorting, trim leading and trailing whitespace from each line, and remove blank lines. This tool is useful for organizing lists of names, keywords, URLs, data entries, and any line-by-line content.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does "numeric" sort do?

Numeric sort treats each line as a number and sorts them in ascending numerical order. Lines that are not valid numbers are placed at the end.

Does this support case-insensitive sort?

Yes. With case insensitive enabled, "Apple" and "apple" are treated equally for sorting purposes, though original casing is preserved in the output.

Is this tool free?

Yes, completely free with no signup required.
