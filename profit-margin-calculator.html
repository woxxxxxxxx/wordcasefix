About Profit Margin Calculator

The Profit Margin Calculator shows four key metrics from revenue and cost: gross profit (revenue minus cost), gross margin percentage (profit divided by revenue), markup percentage (profit divided by cost), and cost as a percentage of revenue. A visual bar chart shows the proportion of cost vs. profit within the total revenue. Both markup and margin are shown together for complete analysis.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is gross profit margin?

Gross margin = (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue x 100. It shows what percentage of revenue remains after covering the cost of goods sold.

What is a good profit margin?

It varies by industry. Retail margins may be 5-20%, while software or services may be 60-80%.
