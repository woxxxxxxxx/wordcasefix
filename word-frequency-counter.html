About the Word Frequency Counter Tool

The Word Frequency Counter analyzes any text and shows how often each word appears. It displays total word count, number of unique words, average word length, and a detailed frequency table with visual distribution bars. Sort words by frequency or alphabetically to find the most common terms, spot keyword density, and analyze writing patterns. This free online word frequency analyzer is essential for SEO professionals optimizing content for search engines, writers checking for overused words, students analyzing literary texts, and researchers studying writing patterns and vocabulary usage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Word Frequency Counter?

A word frequency counter is a free online tool that analyzes text and shows how often each word appears, helping you understand keyword density and writing patterns.

How do I use this Word Frequency Counter?

Paste or type your text into the input area and click Analyze. The tool will display total word count, unique words, average word length, and a detailed frequency table.

Is this Word Frequency Counter free?

Yes, this tool is completely free to use with no signup or login required.
