Frequently Asked Questions

Is Text Statistics free to use?

Yes, completely free. Text Statistics requires no account, no subscription, and no payment. Use it as often as you like with no limits.

Is there a character or word limit in Text Statistics?

No. Text Statistics handles text of any length. Paste in as much content as you need and the tool processes it all instantly.

Does Text Statistics work in my browser without installation?

Yes. Text Statistics runs entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. No software, extension, or plugin installation is required.

Is my text kept private when I use Text Statistics?

Yes. All text processing in Text Statistics happens locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to or stored on any server.

Can I use Text Statistics on a mobile device?

Yes. Text Statistics is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets as well as desktop computers.
