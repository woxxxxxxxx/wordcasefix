About the IP Address Converter

This free IP Address Converter transforms IPv4 addresses between all common formats: dotted decimal (192.168.1.1), 32-bit integer (3232235777), hexadecimal (C0A80101), and binary (11000000.10101000.00000001.00000001). It also shows the IP class and whether the address is private or public. Useful for networking, firewall rules, and low-level programming.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are private IP address ranges?

Private (RFC 1918) ranges are: 10.0.0.0/8 (10.x.x.x), 172.16.0.0/12 (172.16–31.x.x), and 192.168.0.0/16 (192.168.x.x). These are not routable on the public internet and are used in local networks.
