About the Line Counter

This free Line Counter tool gives you instant statistics about your text, including the total number of lines, words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, and paragraphs. Simply paste or type your text and see the counts update live. It's perfect for writers, developers, students, and anyone who needs to quickly analyze the structure of a piece of text. The "exclude empty lines" option helps get accurate non-blank line counts when dealing with spaced-out content.

Frequently Asked Questions

How are lines counted?

Each newline character creates a new line. A single line of text with no newlines counts as 1 line. An empty document counts as 1 line (the current line).

How are words counted?

Words are sequences of non-whitespace characters separated by spaces, tabs, or newlines. Punctuation attached to words is counted as part of the word.

What is the difference between characters with and without spaces?

"Characters" counts every character including spaces and newlines. "No spaces" counts only non-whitespace characters, giving the raw letter and symbol count.

Is this tool free?

Yes, completely free with no signup required.
