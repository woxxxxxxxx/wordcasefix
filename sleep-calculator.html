Sleep Calculator

Find the optimal wake-up times based on 90-minute sleep cycles, or calculate your ideal bedtime.

Average time to fall asleep is 14 minutes -- this is factored in automatically.

Each sleep cycle lasts approximately 90 minutes. Waking up at the end of a cycle (rather than mid-cycle) leaves you feeling more refreshed. 5-6 full cycles (7.5-9 hours) is recommended for most adults.
