About the Typing Speed Test Tool

The Typing Speed Test is a free online tool that measures how fast and accurately you type. A random passage is displayed and you type it character by character. The tool tracks your Words Per Minute (WPM), Characters Per Minute (CPM), accuracy percentage, and elapsed time in real time as you type. Each correctly typed character turns green and errors are highlighted in red. After completing the passage, you get a final summary of your performance including a speed rating. This typing test is ideal for job seekers preparing for typing assessments, students improving their keyboarding skills, writers wanting to boost productivity, and anyone curious about their typing speed. Regular practice with this typing speed checker can help you identify areas for improvement and track your progress over time.
