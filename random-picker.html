About the Random Name Picker Tool

The Random Name Picker is a free online tool that randomly selects names or items from a list you provide. Simply type or paste your list with one item per line, choose how many winners to pick, and click the button. The tool instantly displays the randomly selected item with a fun spinning animation. You can allow or prevent duplicate selections, shuffle the entire list, and reset the picker at any time. This random picker is perfect for teachers selecting students for classroom activities, contest organizers drawing giveaway winners, team captains picking members, and anyone who needs a fair, unbiased random selection. Unlike physical methods like drawing names from a hat, this online random selector ensures truly random results every time.
