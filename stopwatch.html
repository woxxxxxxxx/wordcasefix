About the Stopwatch Tool

The Stopwatch is a free online time-tracking tool that measures elapsed time with precision and supports lap recording. Start and stop the timer, record multiple lap times, and automatically identify your best and worst laps. The stopwatch displays running time clearly and is perfect for fitness training, productivity tracking, cooking, sports practice, and any activity that requires accurate timing. Unlike a countdown timer that counts down to zero, this stopwatch counts up from zero, making it ideal for measuring how long activities take. The lap recording feature lets you track split times during interval training or break down a longer activity into segments for analysis.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Stopwatch?

A stopwatch is a free online tool that measures elapsed time with start, stop, and lap recording functionality for timing activities, sports, and events.

How do I use this Stopwatch?

Click Start to begin timing, Stop to pause, and Reset to clear. Use the Lap button to record split times while the timer continues running.

Is this Stopwatch free?

Yes, this tool is completely free to use with no signup required.
