About the Invisible Text Generator

This free Invisible Text Generator creates Unicode invisible characters that appear as blank space but are actual characters your device can copy, paste, and send. These include the Zero-Width Space (U+200B), Zero-Width Non-Joiner (U+200C), Zero-Width Joiner (U+200D), Word Joiner (U+2060), Braille Blank (U+2800), and the Hangul Filler (U+3164). They are useful for sending blank messages on WhatsApp, creating invisible usernames, adding spacing in social media bios, and bypassing character filters.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a zero-width space?

A zero-width space (U+200B) is a Unicode character that takes up no visible width. It is technically a character but renders as nothing on screen. Some platforms detect it while others ignore it completely.

Does this work on WhatsApp and Instagram?

Yes. The Braille Blank (U+2800) and Hangul Filler (U+3164) work well on WhatsApp for sending blank messages. The Zero-Width Space is effective on Instagram bios. Results may vary as platforms update their filters.

Can I see the invisible characters?

No — that is their purpose. The preview box below the copy button contains the generated characters but they are invisible. The character count will confirm how many were generated.
