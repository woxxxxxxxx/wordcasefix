About the GPA Calculator Tool

The GPA Calculator is a free online tool that computes your Grade Point Average based on your course grades and credit hours. Add each course with its grade letter and credit value, choose between 4.0 and 5.0 scales, and optionally enable weighted GPA for honors or AP courses. This GPA calculator is essential for high school students tracking their academic standing for college applications, college undergraduates monitoring their semester and cumulative GPA, graduate students ensuring they meet minimum GPA requirements, and parents helping their children plan academic goals. The tool automatically calculates both your GPA and total credits, giving you a clear picture of your academic performance.
