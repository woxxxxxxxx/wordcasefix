About the Invoice Generator Tool

The Invoice Generator is a free online tool that helps freelancers, small business owners, and independent contractors create professional invoices quickly. Add your invoice number, date, client name, and line items with descriptions, quantities, and prices. The tool automatically calculates line totals, subtotal, tax amount, and grand total. You can adjust the tax rate as needed and add or remove items at any time. Once complete, simply print the invoice as a PDF using your browser's print function. This invoice maker is ideal for freelance designers, developers, writers, consultants, and anyone who needs to bill clients professionally without investing in expensive accounting software. The clean, printable layout ensures your invoices look polished and business-ready.
