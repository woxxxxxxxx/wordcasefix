About the Case Converter Tool

The Case Converter is a free online tool that transforms text between five different letter case formats: UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, Sentence case, and alternating case. Whether you need to fix improperly formatted text, prepare content for publication, or quickly switch between styles for social media posts, this case converter handles it instantly. Simply paste or type your text and select the desired case transformation. This tool is especially useful for writers, editors, students, and professionals who work with text formatting daily. Unlike word processors that only offer basic case changes, this online case converter provides multiple options in one place, saving you time and eliminating the need to retype content.
