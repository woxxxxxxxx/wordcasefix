About the Tip Calculator Tool

The Tip Calculator is a free online tool that calculates tips and splits bills among multiple people. Enter the bill amount, select a tip percentage from preset options or customize your own, and specify how many people are sharing the bill. The calculator instantly shows the tip amount, total bill including tip, and the amount each person should pay. This tip calculator is perfect for dining out with friends, group travel expenses, business lunch splitting, and any scenario where you need to divide a bill fairly. The preset tip percentages cover common tipping standards, while the custom option lets you enter any percentage for complete flexibility in your gratuity calculations.
