  • Writing for limits: Check length against character or word limits for tweets, meta descriptions, essays, or form fields.
  • Editorial review: Use the Sentence Counter to spot overlong sentences, repeated words, or readability issues before publishing.
  • SEO and content ops: Track keyword counts and density to keep on-page content within target ranges.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Sentence Counter accurate for word and character counts?
Yes. The Sentence Counter follows standard Unicode segmentation rules so counts match what editors and CMS platforms report.
Does the Sentence Counter store any of my text?
No. All analysis happens in your browser and nothing you paste into the Sentence Counter is sent to a server.
Can the Sentence Counter handle non-English text?
Yes. The Sentence Counter supports any language your browser can render, including CJK and right-to-left scripts.
