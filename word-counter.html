Frequently Asked Questions

How are words counted?
The counter splits on whitespace (spaces, tabs, newlines) and treats each non-empty run as one word. Hyphenated terms like "state-of-the-art" count as one word, while em-dash-separated phrases count as two. URLs and numbers also count as single words.
What's a typical reading speed?
Adults read prose at 200-300 words per minute; technical material drops to 50-100 WPM. The counter shows estimated reading time at 225 WPM, the average for online articles. Audiobook narration is slower, around 150-160 WPM.
