About the Word Frequency Counter Tool

The Word Frequency Counter is a free online text analysis tool that counts how often each word appears in a given text and displays the results in a sortable table with an interactive bar chart visualization. It supports case-sensitive counting and includes a stop word filter to exclude common English words like the, and, and of. This word frequency analyzer is invaluable for SEO professionals analyzing keyword density in content, writers checking for overused words, students performing literary analysis, and researchers examining text patterns. The visual bar chart makes it easy to identify the most frequent words at a glance, while the detailed table provides exact counts for precise analysis.
