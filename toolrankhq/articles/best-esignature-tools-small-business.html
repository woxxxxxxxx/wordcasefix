What Makes a Great eSignature Tool for Small Business?

Small businesses have different needs than enterprise companies. You need something that's fast to set up (no IT department), affordable (you're not signing Fortune 500 contracts), legally valid (obviously), and easy for your clients to use (they won't download an app just to sign your contract).

We evaluated each tool on: legal compliance, ease of use, pricing at small business scale, integration capabilities, mobile experience, and audit trail quality.
