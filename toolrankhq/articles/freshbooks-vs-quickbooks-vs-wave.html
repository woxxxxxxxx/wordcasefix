Who Should Use Which?

Use FreshBooks if: You're a freelancer or service business, invoicing is your primary need, you bill by the hour, you have 5–50 active clients, and you want the best UX in the category.

Use QuickBooks if: You have employees, sell physical products (inventory), need multiple user access, want QuickBooks-native payroll, or your accountant specifically uses QuickBooks (common).

Use Wave if: You're on a tight budget, you have simple finances (no employees, no inventory), you can tolerate higher transaction fees, or you're just starting out and not sure what you need yet.

Upgrade from Wave to FreshBooks if: You want the best AI-powered automation for invoicing specifically — smart reminders, late payment follow-up, and time tracking tied directly to invoices.
