How We Tested AI Writing Tools

We ran each tool through the same battery of business writing tasks: write a cold outreach email, create a proposal for a web design project, write a blog post introduction, draft a LinkedIn post, summarize a meeting transcript, and create marketing copy for a product. We evaluated output on quality, accuracy, tone appropriateness, and how much editing was required.

1. Claude — Best for Nuanced Business Writing

Claude (Anthropic)

AI Writing Assistant
9.4
★★★★★
