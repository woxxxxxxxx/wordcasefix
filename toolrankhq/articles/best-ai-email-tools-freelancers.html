How AI Is Transforming Email for Freelancers

The average freelancer spends 2.5 hours per day on email. That's 625 hours per year — roughly 15 working weeks. AI email tools are finally making a dent in this: they can draft replies in your voice, summarize long email threads, prioritize what needs your attention, schedule follow-ups automatically, and unsubscribe from newsletters in bulk.

We tested 5 tools for 6 weeks each, tracking: time spent on email, reply quality, thread summarization accuracy, and how well the AI learned to match individual writing styles.
