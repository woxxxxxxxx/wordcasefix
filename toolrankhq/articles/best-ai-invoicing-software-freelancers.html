What to Look for in AI Invoicing Software

Late payments are the #1 cash flow problem for freelancers. The right invoicing software solves this not just by sending invoices, but by automating reminders, tracking payment status, and making it dead easy for clients to pay. AI-powered tools go further — they can predict which clients are likely to pay late, suggest optimal payment terms, and auto-generate invoice line items from time tracking data.

We evaluated 7 tools on: invoice creation speed, payment processing options and fees, automated reminders, recurring billing, expense tracking, tax calculations, and overall value for solo freelancers.
