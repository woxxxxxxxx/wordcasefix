About WordCaseFix

Our Mission

WordCaseFix was created to provide a comprehensive collection of free, easy-to-use online tools that help people accomplish everyday tasks quickly and efficiently. From text conversion and word counting to password generation and unit conversion, we aim to cover the most commonly needed utilities in one convenient place.

We believe that essential online tools should be free, fast, and respect your privacy. That is why all of our tools process data entirely in your browser -- nothing you type or upload is ever sent to our servers.
