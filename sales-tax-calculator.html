About Sales Tax Calculator

The Sales Tax Calculator computes the tax amount and final total price given a pre-tax price and tax rate. It also works in reverse: enter the total price (including tax) and the tax rate to find the original pre-tax price and tax amount. This is useful for shopping, invoicing, accounting, and financial planning.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is sales tax calculated?

Sales tax = Price x (Tax Rate / 100). The total is Price + Tax.

How do I find the pre-tax price from a total?

Pre-Tax Price = Total / (1 + Tax Rate / 100). The tax amount is Total - Pre-Tax Price.

Is this tool free?

Yes, completely free with no login required.
