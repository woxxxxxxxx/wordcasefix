About the Text Diff Checker Tool

The Text Diff Checker is a free online tool that compares two blocks of text and highlights the differences between them. Paste your original text and modified text side by side, then click compare to see added lines highlighted in green and removed lines highlighted in red. Options for case-sensitive comparison and line trimming give you fine control over the diff process. This diff checker is invaluable for writers comparing document revisions, developers reviewing code changes, editors proofreading content, and anyone who needs to see exactly what changed between two versions of text. The clear visual highlighting makes it easy to spot even small changes across large documents.
