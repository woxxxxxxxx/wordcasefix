About the Roman Numeral Converter Tool

The Roman Numeral Converter is a free online tool that converts between Roman numerals and Arabic numbers. Convert any number from 1 to 3999 into its Roman numeral representation (I, V, X, L, C, D, M), or translate Roman numerals back into standard numbers. The converter follows standard Roman numeral rules including subtractive notation (IV for 4, IX for 9, XL for 40, XC for 90, CD for 400, CM for 900). Quick conversion buttons let you instantly see the Roman equivalents of common values, and the built-in reference chart shows how each digit position is represented. This Roman numeral translator is essential for students learning Roman numerals in history or Latin class, historians and researchers working with ancient texts and inscriptions, event planners formatting copyright years and movie release dates, and anyone curious about converting birth years or special numbers into Roman numerals for tattoos, jewelry, or decorative designs.
