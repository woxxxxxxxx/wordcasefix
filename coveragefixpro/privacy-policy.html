Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

CoverageFixPro ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website coveragefixpro.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our free insurance calculators. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Enter into Calculators

All calculator inputs (such as age, income, vehicle type, or property value) are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We do not transmit, store, or log any values you enter into our calculators on our servers.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information including: browser type and version, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, time and date of visit, and general geographic location (country/region level only). This information is collected via Google Analytics and is used solely to understand how visitors use our site and to improve our content.

Cookies

We use cookies for analytics and advertising purposes. These include:

  • Google Analytics cookies — to analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Google AdSense cookies — to serve relevant advertisements

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

2. How We Use Information

We use the automatically collected information to:

  • Analyze and improve website performance and content
  • Understand which calculators are most useful to visitors
  • Display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • Ensure website security and prevent abuse

3. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other sites on the internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

We do not control the content of third-party advertisements and are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party advertisers.

4. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or financial account details through our website.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Data Security

Since our calculators do not transmit your inputs to any server, the data you enter remains on your device. We implement reasonable technical measures to protect our website from unauthorized access, but no internet transmission is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@coveragefixpro.com
