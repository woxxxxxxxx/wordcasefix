### home/home-insurance-deductible-calculator.html [容器: .info-box]

    <h2>How to Choose Your Deductible</h2>
    <p>Your deductible is the amount you pay out of pocket before insurance kicks in. Higher deductibles lower your annual premium but increase your out-of-pocket cost at claim time. The optimal deductible depends on your claim history, risk tolerance, and emergency savings. Generally, if you rarely file claims, a higher deductible saves money long-term. If you live in a high-risk area for weather events, a lower deductible may be worth the added premium cost. Always choose a deductible you can comfortably afford to pay.</p>
