About CoverageFixPro

Our Mission

CoverageFixPro is a free online resource providing 120+ insurance calculators and tools to help individuals and families make smarter coverage decisions. We believe that understanding insurance doesn't require a degree — it requires clear, accessible tools and honest information.

Our calculators cover five major insurance categories: auto, health, life, home, and business. Every tool runs entirely in your browser — no account, no signup, and no personal data collected.

Data Sources & Methodology

Our calculators are built using publicly available data including:

  • IRS guidelines for HSA, FSA, and tax deduction limits
  • CMS (Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services) published rate tables
  • Federal Register ACA affordability thresholds
  • Industry average rate multipliers from NAIC reports
  • Federal Poverty Level guidelines updated for 2026

All figures are reviewed and updated annually to reflect the latest regulatory changes.
