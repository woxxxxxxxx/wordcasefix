Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ACA premium tax credit? ▼
The premium tax credit (PTC) subsidizes health insurance costs for people with incomes between 100% and 400% of the federal poverty level. Our ACA Subsidy Calculator estimates your eligibility and credit amount.
What is the difference between HMO and PPO? ▼
HMOs require you to use network providers and get referrals. PPOs offer more flexibility to see out-of-network providers at higher cost. Our HMO vs PPO Calculator shows which plan costs less for your situation.
When can I enroll in a health insurance plan? ▼
Outside of the ACA Open Enrollment period (November-January), you can enroll during a Special Enrollment Period triggered by life events like job loss, marriage, birth, or moving.
