Frequently Asked Questions

How are auto insurance rates calculated? ▼
Insurers use your age, driving record, vehicle year and model, annual mileage, location, and credit score to set rates. Our calculators model the most important factors to give you a realistic estimate range.
What is the minimum auto insurance required? ▼
Every state requires at least liability coverage. Most states require bodily injury liability (e.g. $25,000/$50,000) and property damage liability (e.g. $10,000). Some states also require uninsured motorist coverage or PIP.
How can I lower my car insurance premium? ▼
Bundle home and auto, raise your deductible, maintain a clean driving record, take a defensive driving course, and ask about multi-car discounts. Our Bundling Discount Calculator shows exactly how much you can save.
