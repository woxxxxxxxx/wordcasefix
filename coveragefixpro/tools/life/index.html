Frequently Asked Questions

How much life insurance do I need? ▼
A common rule of thumb is 10-12× your annual income, but the right amount depends on your debts, dependents, income replacement needs, and existing assets. Use our Life Insurance Needs Calculator for a personalized estimate.
What is the difference between term and whole life insurance? ▼
Term life provides coverage for a set period (10-30 years) at lower cost. Whole life covers you for life and builds cash value. Our Term vs Whole Life Calculator compares total costs and benefits.
Can I get life insurance if I'm a smoker? ▼
Yes, but smokers typically pay 2-4× more than non-smokers for the same coverage. Quitting for 12 months before applying can significantly reduce your rates.
