About the Percentage Calculator Tool

The Percentage Calculator offers three calculation modes to cover every percentage problem. Mode 1 calculates what X percent of Y equals. Mode 2 finds what percentage X is of Y. Mode 3 calculates the percentage change (increase or decrease) between two values. All calculations update in real time as you type.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate X% of Y?

Enter X in the percent field and Y in the value field. The result updates automatically.

How do I find percentage change?

Switch to the "% Change" mode, enter the starting value in X and the ending value in Y. The tool shows the percentage increase or decrease.

Is this tool free?

Yes, completely free.
