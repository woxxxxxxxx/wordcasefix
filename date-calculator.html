About the Date Duration Calculator Tool

The Date Duration Calculator is a free online tool that computes the exact number of days, months, and years between two dates. It also supports adding or subtracting days from a specific date, making it a versatile tool for all your date math needs. Use the business days option to count only weekdays, excluding Saturdays and Sundays, which is particularly useful for project planning, contract timelines, and work schedules. This date calculator is essential for event planning, tracking project deadlines, calculating age in days, planning vacations, determining invoice due dates, and any situation requiring precise date arithmetic. Unlike manually counting days on a calendar, this online date duration calculator gives you instant, accurate results with just a few clicks.
