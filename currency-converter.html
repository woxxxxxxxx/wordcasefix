About the Currency Converter Tool

The Currency Converter is a free online tool for converting between 20 major world currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CNY, and more. It uses a static reference rate table with rates against the US Dollar for quick and easy conversions. The swap button lets you instantly reverse the currency pair. This free online currency converter is useful for travelers, international shoppers, business professionals, and anyone who needs to quickly convert between different currencies for reference purposes.
