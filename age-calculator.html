About the Age Calculator Tool

The Age Calculator is a free online tool that computes your exact age in years, months, days, hours, and minutes based on your date of birth. It also shows your next birthday countdown and the total time you have lived since birth. Simply select your birth date using the date picker, and the calculator instantly displays your precise age. This age calculator is perfect for birthday planning, milestone tracking, filling out forms that require exact age, or simply satisfying your curiosity about how many days old you are. Unlike basic age calculators that only show years, this tool provides a comprehensive breakdown of your age across multiple time units, giving you a complete picture of your time spent on earth.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Age Calculator?

An age calculator is a free online tool that computes your exact age in years, months, days, hours, and minutes based on your date of birth. It also shows your next birthday countdown and total time lived since birth.

How do I use this Age Calculator?

Simply select your date of birth using the date picker, optionally choose an end date, and click Calculate Age. The tool instantly displays your precise age in multiple time units along with your next birthday countdown.

Is this Age Calculator free?

Yes, this tool is completely free to use with no signup required.
