About the Calorie Calculator Tool

The Calorie Calculator is a free online tool that estimates your daily calorie requirements using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, one of the most accurate BMR formulas. Simply enter your age, gender, weight, height, and activity level to calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE). The results show your maintenance calories as well as calorie targets for mild, moderate, and extreme weight loss or gain. This calorie needs calculator is essential for fitness enthusiasts planning their meal prep and macros, people trying to lose, maintain, or gain weight with a science-based approach, athletes optimizing their nutrition for performance, and healthcare professionals counseling clients on nutrition. Understanding your TDEE is the foundation of any effective diet or muscle-building plan.
