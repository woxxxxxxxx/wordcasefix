About the Password Generator Tool

This free online password generator creates strong, random passwords to help protect your online accounts. You can customize the length from 8 to 64 characters and choose which character sets to include: uppercase letters, lowercase letters, digits, and symbols. The tool also lets you generate up to 10 passwords at once, so you can pick your favorite or use different passwords across multiple accounts. Each generated password includes a strength indicator and can be copied to your clipboard with a single click. All password generation happens entirely in your browser - nothing is sent to any server, keeping your potential passwords completely private and secure.
