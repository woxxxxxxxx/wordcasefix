Frequently Asked Questions

What formula does the Calculator use?
The calculator implements the standard published formula for this domain. Enter your values, and the result reflects an exact computation rounded to two decimal places. Hover the result for the full-precision value before rounding.
Are results accurate enough for tax or legal filings?
The calculator is accurate to the formula but cannot replace certified professional advice. Tax law, insurance underwriting, and regulatory compliance involve case-by-case factors. Use the output as a planning estimate, then verify with a licensed accountant or attorney.
Can I save scenarios for later comparison?
The calculator runs entirely in your browser. Use the print or copy button to save a snapshot, or take a screenshot to compare alongside alternative scenarios. There's no account or cloud storage; your inputs aren't logged.
