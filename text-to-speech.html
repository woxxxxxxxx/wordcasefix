About the Text to Speech Tool

The Text to Speech tool is a free online TTS (text-to-speech) converter that reads any text aloud using your browser's built-in speech synthesis. Type or paste any text, choose from available voices, adjust the speaking speed and pitch, and click play to listen. This text reader is perfect for students who want to listen to study materials, writers proofreading their work by ear, people with reading difficulties or visual impairments, language learners improving pronunciation and listening skills, and anyone who prefers listening over reading. Since everything runs locally in your browser using the Web Speech API, no text is ever sent to a server -- your privacy is fully protected. The online text reader supports multiple languages and voices depending on your operating system and browser.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Text to Speech?

A text to speech tool is a free online tool that converts written text into spoken audio using browser-based speech synthesis for accessibility and convenience.

How do I use this Text to Speech?

Type or paste text into the input area, choose a voice and speech rate if desired, then click Play. The tool reads the text aloud using your browser built-in speech synthesis.

Is this Text to Speech free?

Yes, this tool is completely free to use with no signup required.
