About the Aspect Ratio Calculator

This free Aspect Ratio Calculator computes the aspect ratio from any width and height, reduces it to its simplest form (e.g., 1920×1080 → 16:9), and lets you scale dimensions while maintaining the ratio. Click any common ratio chip to see its typical resolution.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is aspect ratio?

Aspect ratio is the proportional relationship between width and height. A 16:9 ratio means for every 16 units wide the image is 9 units tall. Common ratios: 16:9 (widescreen), 4:3 (traditional TV), 1:1 (square), 9:16 (portrait/mobile).
