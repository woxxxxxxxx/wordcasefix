Does the Countdown Timer keep running if I switch tabs?
Yes. The Countdown Timer uses timestamps rather than tab focus, so elapsed time stays accurate in the background.
Will the Countdown Timer play a sound when finished?
Yes, where supported. The Countdown Timer plays an audible alert when the session ends as long as your browser allows it.
Can I keep the Countdown Timer visible on screen?
Yes. Resize the window or use your browser's picture-in-picture or always-on-top mode to keep it visible.
