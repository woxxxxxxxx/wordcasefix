About the Countdown Timer Tool

The Countdown Timer is a free online tool that counts down from a specified amount of time and alerts you when the time is up. Set hours, minutes, and seconds using the input fields, or choose from quick preset buttons including 1 minute, 5 minutes, 10 minutes, 15 minutes, 30 minutes, and 1 hour. The large digital display shows the remaining time clearly, and an alarm message appears when the countdown reaches zero. This online timer is perfect for cooking, workout intervals, study sessions, presentation timing, meditation, and any activity that requires time management. Unlike a stopwatch that measures elapsed time, this countdown timer helps you work within a defined time boundary.
