About the Character Counter Tool

The Character Counter is a free online tool that provides instant text analysis, counting characters with and without spaces, total words, sentences, letters, and numeric digits. Unlike basic word counters that only track word count, this character counter breaks down the exact composition of your text. It is ideal for writers crafting social media posts with strict character limits, students meeting essay length requirements, SEO professionals optimizing meta descriptions, and content creators who need precise text metrics. The tool updates in real time as you type, giving you immediate feedback on your text structure and length.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Character Counter?

A character counter is a free online tool that counts characters with and without spaces, words, sentences, letters, and numbers in any text for writers and content creators.

How do I use this Character Counter?

Type or paste your text into the text area. The tool automatically updates all character, word, and sentence counts in real time as you type.

Is this Character Counter free?

Yes, this tool is completely free to use with no signup required.
