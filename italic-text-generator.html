About the Italic Text Generator

This free Italic Text Generator uses Unicode Mathematical Alphanumeric Symbols to create text that looks italic in any app or website — no HTML formatting needed. Because these are actual Unicode characters (not formatting codes), they work in Instagram bios, Twitter posts, Facebook updates, Discord, Slack, WhatsApp, and any other platform that supports standard Unicode text. Three variants are generated: Mathematical Italic (serif), Mathematical Italic Sans-Serif, and Bold Italic.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why doesn't every character have an italic version?

Unicode italic covers most Latin letters. Some special characters like digits do not have italic equivalents, so they appear as regular characters in the output.

Where can I use italic Unicode text?

Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook, Discord, Slack, Telegram, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, and any app that supports Unicode text rendering.
